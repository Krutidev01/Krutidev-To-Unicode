Why Unicode Matters

Legacy fonts like Kruti Dev are invisible to Google. Unicode (Mangal) allows your content to be indexed, translated, and voice-searched. It is the only standard accepted by the Government of India for official digital communication.

The Technical Shift

For decades, the Indian administrative ecosystem relied on Kruti Dev. It was a clever workaround for the pre-internet era, mapping Hindi glyphs to English keys. However, this creates "Dead Data"—information that cannot be processed by modern AI or databases.

The solution is Kruti Dev to Unicode Conversion. This process involves a complex algorithmic translation that reorders "Matras" (vowels) and joins split characters into single semantic units.

Photoshop & Design Tip

If you are pasting converted text into Adobe Photoshop and the vowels look broken, change your text engine settings: Edit > Preferences > Type > World Ready Layout.
